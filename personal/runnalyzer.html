Training and Racing with Purpose

Data Driven

  • Monitor improvement: Tracking heart rate (BPM) and cadence (SPM) helps visualize gains in endurance, efficiency, and recovery over time.
  • Balance training and rest: Data shows when to push harder or scale back, preventing overtraining and aiding recovery.
  • Detect issues early: Sudden changes in BPM or SPM can highlight fatigue, poor form, or the need for adjustments in your plan.
