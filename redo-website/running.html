How I got into Running

Philly Marathon Running Vlog!

Inspiration

I got into running after watching my dad complete three marathons (and counting) at what many would call an "old age"—it was both inspiring and humbling. Seeing him push his limits made me believe I could do the same, no matter when I started.

Setbacks

Two years ago, I signed up for my first marathon but had to pull out due to a sciatica injury during training. It was a tough setback, but it left me more motivated than ever to come back stronger.

Goals

Fast forward to this past year, I was able to finish my first marathon, exceeding my own expectations. Since then, I've made a commitment to improving my health and wellbeing through consistent exercise—I've already signed up for next year's marathon and plan to run a few half marathons along the way.
